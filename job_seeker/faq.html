Frequently Asked Questions

For Job Seekers

How does the assessment process work?
▼

You’ll complete an assessment comprising a series of questions designed to understand your skills, experiences, and preferences. Based on your answers, our system generates a detailed profile highlighting your strengths.

Is the assessment free to take?
▼

Yes, the assessment is completely free for job seekers.

Can I pause the assessment and resume later?
▼

Yes, you can save your progress and resume the assessment at any time.

Will my profile be automatically updated with my new assessment results?
▼

Yes, once you complete a new assessment, your profile will be updated automatically to reflect the latest results.

How often should I update my profile?
▼

We recommend updating your profile every six months or whenever you acquire new skills or experience to keep it relevant and up-to-date.

Is my data safe on your platform?
▼

Absolutely, we take data security seriously and use advanced encryption methods to protect your information.

For Employers

How can I view candidate profiles?
▼

After registering and logging in as an employer, you’ll have access to our database of candidate profiles. You can search for candidates based on various criteria like skills, experience, and assessment results.

Is there a fee to access candidate profiles?
▼

Access to candidate profiles is completely free of charge.

How do I contact candidates I’m interested in?
▼

You can either contact them via a third-party app or send messages directly through our platform to candidates you’re interested in. They will receive a notification and can respond via the platform.

What type of assessments do candidates complete?
▼

Candidates complete a comprehensive assessment that evaluates their skills, experiences, and job preferences. The results help you identify the best matches for your job openings.

How can I edit my company profile?
▼

You can edit your company profile by logging into your employer account and navigating to the “Profile” section, where you can update your company information, logo, and description.

How often is your candidate database updated?
▼

Our candidate database is continuously updated as new job seekers join and existing profiles are refreshed.
